# Isoform Uncertainty Completion Evidence

Isoform uncertainty remains complete when:

- scientific outputs are unchanged
- immutable scientific artifacts are written once
- metadata records are finalized once after immutable checksums exist
- run/result metadata does not contain unverifiable self-checksums
- pre-commit verification passes
- post-commit verification passes
- corrupted committed output is not reused
- exact coverage evidence is recorded from final source

The completed workflow stage remains separate from transcript targetability, N, M, M/N, intended-target calibration, direct effect, residual, secondary effect, and classification.
